About Me

Hello! I'm Gayatri Ghorpade, a motivated Computer Engineering student from Pune, Maharashtra. I have hands-on experience with web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Django. I'm passionate about building functional and user-friendly websites and constantly improving my technical and soft skills.

I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree at Dr. D.Y. Patil College of Engineering and Innovation, after completing my Diploma in Computer Engineering from Y. B. Patil Polytechnic.

I love learning and building things on the web. I got into coding because I enjoy figuring out how things work and making ideas come to life online.

I've worked on websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and also explored backend development with Django during my internship. I like creating clean, simple designs that work well and are easy to use.

Outside of coding, I'm always trying to improve how I communicate and work with people — whether it’s in a team or just learning something new. I believe that being curious and open to learning is just as important as technical skills.
